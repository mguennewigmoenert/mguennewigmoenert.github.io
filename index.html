Maximilian Günnewig-Mönert

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Center for Economic Research at the University of Cologne. My research aims to contribute to our understanding of what drives the (unequal) distribution of people and economic activity across space, with a focus on the role of social housing and local labor markets. I received my PhD from Trinity College Dublin in 2024.
